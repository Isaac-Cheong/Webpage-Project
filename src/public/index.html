Our Story

Born from a love of bold flavors and joy of sharing great food, our restaurant brings the vibrant tradition of mala cuisine to life. Inspired by the fiery origins of mala xiang guo from the mountains of Chongqing, our journey began with a simple mission: to create a place where friends and families could gather around a table, choose their favorite ingredients, and savor the numbing, spicy magic that defines this beloved dish
